❖ ----- Biographie ----- ❖

Born Yoko, Vendredi once lived in a village near Old Kamura with her twin sister, Tsuki. Yoko was known for being energetic, hot-headed and mischievous, while Tsuki was quieter and deeply caring.

Their world was shattered on a Friday when their home was struck by the Calamity, where monsters razed the village. Yoko and Tsuki were buried under debris for ten hours, and ultimately, they were the only survivors of their village. Rescued by a kind Hunter, the twins followed him to the Guild and trained to become hunters themselves, quickly growing into a fearsome duo known as the Sun and Moon.

This partnership tragically ended about five years ago during a hunt they should not have taken: when Yoko acted recklessly and missed a step, Tsuki shielded her sister from a fatal blow and died, making Yoko promise to live for both of them.

Broken and unable to function as a hunter, Yoko eventually left the Guild, traveling the world, training with legends, and picking up new weapons, until she eventually changed her name, look, and identity to become Vendredi in order to fulfill her vow and find herself again. Even now, Vendredi maintains raven-black braided twintails streaked with purple, an homage to Tsuki.

Years later, a summons arrives: the Guild mounts an expedition into the Forbidden Lands, and Vendredi is chosen for the Alvis Unit. She accepts to guide Nata, a boy returning to the ruins of his monster-ravaged village.

With the Alvis Unit, Vendredi crosses into new frontiers alongside the Astrum Unit and local allies. She fells each region’s apex—Rey Dau, Uth Duna, Nu Udra, and Jin Dahaad—while uncovering Wyveria’s lost designs, the lingering threat of the Arkveld, and the world-shaping role of the Dragon Torch. More than once she cuts down the Zoh Shia, a beast she loathes for the memories it drags up.
